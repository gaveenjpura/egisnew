About EGIS

Launched in 2018, EGIS is the leading GIS based E-Commerce platform in world. We serve millions of buyers, sellers and suppliers around the Sri Lanka.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make it easy to do business anywhere in Sri Lanka getting help of GIS technologies.
We do this by giving sellers, suppliers the tools necessary to reach a audience for their products and helping buyers to find products and sellers quickly and efficiency.

One-Stop Sourcing

EGIS brings you thousands of products in major categories including consumer electronics, machinery and apparel.
Buyers and sellers are located in 9 provinces and thousands of transactions happen on the platform each day.

Anytime,Anywhere

As a platform, we continue to develop services to help businesses do more and discover new opportunities.

Branches

EGIS main office is located at Bambalapitiya,Colombo 04. Other branches of EGIS are located at Kurunegala, Kandy, Galle.
