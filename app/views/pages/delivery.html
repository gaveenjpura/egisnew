EGIS Delivery Services - Frequently Asked Questions

What about the geographic scope of EGIS product delivery?
Product Delivery of EGIS is limited within Sri Lanka (all 9 provinces).
What are available delivery options in EGIS?
There are two available delivery options in EGIS.
  • EGIS Product Delivery Service
  • Third Party Logistic Services
Do you deliver on weekends and public holidays?
Yes. EGIS delivery service provides their serivces on weekends and holidays. To Know about other third party services please contact relevant third party logistic companies.
How do I track my product delivery?
You can track your product up to last mile of delivery by using our real time synchronized good tracking service.
What is the earliest that my orders can be delivered in Sri Lanka ?
You need to place the order at least one day prior to the delivery date.
Is the delivery guaranteed in Sri Lanka ?
Yes absolutely. We are responsible for the EGIS product delivery service. But we are not responsible for any third party logistic services. But sellers are responsible to report to EGIS for product delivery.
